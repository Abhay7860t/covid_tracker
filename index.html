HOW TO PREVENT VIRUS

Clean Your Place

Always try to keep your surroundings and your house clean.

Maintain Distance

Always try to maintain a proper distance from people.

Dont't Touch Face

Don't touch your face because it can spread virus even faster by touching it.

Wash Your Hand

Always wash your hands whenever you come to your home from outside. It can prevent virus at some extent.

Use Napkin

Usage of Napkins can be used while cough or sneeze to prevent infection.

Wear A Mask

It is a best practice to wear a mask whenever you step out of your home.It helps to keep an individual safe from virus.
